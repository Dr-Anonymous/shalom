Our Ministries

With a vision to serve the body of Christ, reach the perishing souls, and to cater their needs - worship services, children's ministry, youth ministry and special prayers are envisioned.

Worship Service:

Every Sunday from 10:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m., we have a worship service at our church. We share testimonies, offer our thanksgiving, study His word and pray for any of those amongst us with needs. On the first Sunday of every month, we also have communion service.

Online Ministry:

We believe in using all the ways at our disposal to spread God's word. We began our online and digital ministries from 2014 with an aim to deliver God's word to those of our members who have migrated to other places. This is done through various channels like this website, an audio sermon library, a Facebook page for daily word and a YouTube channel for videos. With the advent of COVID pandemic, we have also started online live sessions of our Friday and Sunday services.

Children's Ministry:

"Train a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not turn from it." -Pro 22:6

Every Sunday (during the traditional worship service timings) children are taken care of by teachers who impart moral and spiritual values through Bible stories that helps them to grow in the fear and favour of the Lord. Occasionally they take up cultural activities furthering their God given talents to the joy of their parents and the church.

Special Prayers:

Every Friday evening (7 to 9:00 p.m.) we have fasting prayers for the new comers, for those who have prayer needs, and to meditate on God's word so that we as a church would stand as an effective witness for our Lord. Many wonderful testimonies have come up by God's grace as a result of our prayers.

Brothers' Fellowship:

Once a month brothers come together for 'fellowship' time where they discuss the ministerial issues, plan the upcoming events, share the responsibilities of church activities, account to one another and pray for the growth of our ministry.
