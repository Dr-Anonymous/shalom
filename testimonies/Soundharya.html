GOD'S LOVE FORGIVES

- Soundharya

         I am ESTHER SOUNDHARYA MADDUKURI. I'm a graduate in Medicine. I come from a Christ devoted family and was brought up with good spiritual values right from my childhood. I grew in The Lord throughout my schooling. But, after I entered my college and medical school I have gone far away from The Lord. I acted very much contrary to His Word. The Lord spoke to me in many ways but I remained disobedient. In spite of my disobedience The Lord did not leave me.
        In the year 2011 He started dealing with me. He not only spoke to me through the preaching of our Pastor but He has also given me a Spirit of realization and obedience. Convicted of my sins I cried out to Him for forgiveness.On April 13th, 2011 The Lord spoke to me personally through the scripture -"When we were utterly helpless, Christ came at just the right time and died for us sinners." Romans 5:6. Then I gave my life to Him, and was baptized on April 20th,2011. Now, I continue to live in The Lord. Not only did He bless me in all aspects of my life but also by His Grace He has enabled me to take part in His Ministry.
Glory Be To God.
