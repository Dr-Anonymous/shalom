BREAKING THE CHAINS OF FRUITLESSNESS....

- Narendra

          I am Narendra Kumpatla.I take it a pleasure to share my testimony with you. Our God is a wonderful God who does miracles in the lives of those who believe Him.

        My wife conceived twice in 2005 and 2007 but due to uncontrolled gestational diabetes that remained resistant to treatment we lost two children at delivery time. That was the most difficult and painful time in our lives and we were in a depressed state. But our gracious God comforted us through His word and even through the preaching and counseling of our Pastor Prasad garu. We kept our faith in God. God saw the anguish of our hearts and also heard our prayers. Later God blessed us with two children, Shiney and Sherley, one in 2008 and the other in 2012.

          I am truly grateful that the good Lord had used His servant to break the chains of fruitlessness and blessed us with two lovely kids. I thank God for His mercies upon my family and I hope this testimony will be a blessing to all those who read it.
